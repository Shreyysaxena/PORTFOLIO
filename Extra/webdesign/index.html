Web design refers to the process of planning, creating, and maintaining websites. It encompasses various elements, including webpage layout, content production, graphic design, and user experience (UX) design. The goal of web design is to create visually appealing, user-friendly, and effective websites that meet the needs of both the site owner and its users.
